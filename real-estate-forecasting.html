The Challenge

Traditional real estate investment relies heavily on historical data spreadsheets. Our client wanted to leverage their vast dataset to predict future market trends, but the raw data was overwhelming for agents.

[UI Mockup: Interactive Forecast Chart]

The Solution

We designed a "Confidence Interval" interface. Instead of showing a single predicted number, the AI presents a range of probable outcomes. Agents can toggle variables (interest rates, migration patterns) to see how the forecast shifts in real-time.
